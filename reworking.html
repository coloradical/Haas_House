Casey Haas is a filmmaker, photographer, and designer from the Bay Area. She is a recent graduate of CU Boulder & General Assembly, where she received a BFA in Film Production and a certificate in Visual Design. She created Haas House as an umbrella for all of her work- film, photography, and design. Her creations reflect the California dream she grew up in, whether it be the beaches of Half Moon Bay or the park down the street from the home she grew up in. A firm believer in the saying ‘the most personal is the most creative’, her work often explores her childhood and life experiences. Casey specializes in shooting 35mm, 120mm, super 8mm, and 16mm film.
